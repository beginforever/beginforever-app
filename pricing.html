Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Begin Forever

What makes Begin Forever different?v
India's first 100% ID-verified matrimony platform open to all faiths. Every member submits a valid government ID and is manually approved. Zero fake profiles. Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist - Begin Forever is built for you.
How does the verification process work?v
Upload a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving Licence, or Passport). Our admin team manually verifies every profile within 24-48 hours. Your ID is stored securely and never shared with other members.
Is my personal information safe?v
All data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS. Your phone and email are never shown to other members. Photos are private by default. We never sell or share your data. Read our full Privacy Policy.
Can my family help manage my profile?v
Yes! You can register on behalf of your son, daughter, brother, sister, or other relative. Family members can help manage the profile and review matches - because marriage is a commitment that involves families.
What faiths and denominations are supported?v
Begin Forever welcomes all faiths - Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist and others. For Christians, we support all denominations: Catholic, Protestant, Pentecostal, Baptist, CSI/CNI, Methodist, SDA, Orthodox, Mar Thoma, Brethren, Lutheran, Presbyterian, Anglican, Non-Denominational, and more.
When will pricing plans be available?v
Pricing plans will be published at launch. We are working on Basic and Premium tiers. All founding members (first 300) are guaranteed 1 week free on any plan - no matter what the final pricing looks like.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?v
Yes, cancel anytime. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep access until end of your billing period. You can also delete your account entirely from profile settings - all data removed within 30 days.
